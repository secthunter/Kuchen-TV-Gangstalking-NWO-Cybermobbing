# Tom Rohrböck Collected Information

## Biography
Businessman and networker, described as "rechtes Phantom" (right-wing phantom) by researchers from NDR, WDR, and Zeit. Built a network over 10 years reaching to Bavarian Greens. Former Seligenstadt CDU chairman. Denies media reports, calls himself a networker with many contacts.

## Controversies
Dubious consultant influencing AfD since its founding, shifting the republic to the right. AfD leadership alarmed, Bundesvorstand wants to investigate his role. Secret network influencing AfD internally and personnel alignment.
