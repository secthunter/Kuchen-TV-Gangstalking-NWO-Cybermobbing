# Northdata Research

## Company Background
North Data GmbH is a German company providing business data and company information from public sources such as trade registers, annual reports, funding registers, trademark records, patent registries, and others. It uses big data and AI to compile and visualize data. Founded in 2011, headquartered in Hamburg, HRB 116737. The object of the company is the development of internet-based projects and technologies, IT consulting in databases, business information, web crawling, and data mining.
