# Naomi Seibt Collected Information

## Biography
Born August 18, 2000 in Münster, Germany. German far-right political activist, climate change denier, opposes Greta Thunberg. Employed by Heartland Institute (American far-right climate denial group) until April 2020.

## Controversies
Filed for political asylum in the US, claims German intelligence spied on her for years, received death threats from Antifa. Backed by Elon Musk.

Potential anti-Semite, praised white nationalist YouTuber Stefan Molyneux at CPAC 2020.

Supports anti-immigration AfD, labeled extremist by authorities.
